StepCharge Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Summary: StepCharge processes data only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. There is no user account and no cloud backend.

StepCharge (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is an Android application that helps you reduce distracting phone use by linking app access to physical activity. This policy explains how we handle information when you use StepCharge.

Information Processed on Your Device

To provide its features, the app may process the following locally on your phone:

Data Purpose
Step count / motion activity Detect walking and unlock or earn app time
List of installed apps Let you choose which apps to protect
Foreground app usage Detect when a protected app is open
App preferences Protected apps, modes (gate/budget), buffer time, onboarding status
Protection state Whether protection is on, unlock/block timers

This information is stored in your device’s local app storage and is not sent to our servers. We do not operate servers for this app.

Permissions

StepCharge requests Android permissions only to deliver its features:

  • Usage access — detect which app is in the foreground
  • Activity recognition — read step count / movement
  • Display over other apps — show block screen and timer overlays
  • Notifications — show a persistent notification while protection is running
  • Foreground service — monitor apps in the background
  • Boot completed — restart protection after device reboot
  • Battery optimization exemption (optional) — improve reliability of background monitoring

You can revoke permissions in Android Settings at any time. Some features may stop working if required permissions are denied.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or analytics. The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data remains on your device until you:

  • Clear app data in Android Settings → Apps → StepCharge → Storage → Clear data, or
  • Uninstall the app

Uninstalling StepCharge removes locally stored app data from your device.

Children’s Privacy

StepCharge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Data is processed and stored locally on your device using Android’s standard app sandbox. We do not transmit this data over the internet.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will be revised when changes are made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
